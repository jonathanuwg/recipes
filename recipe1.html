Grilled Chicken Recipe

by Jonathan Khamis

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds of thighs
  • 1 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 spoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper ( as desired )
  • Big Ziplock bag

Directions

  1. Except the chicken, put all other ingredients in a ziplock bag and mix them well until sugar and salt are dissolved into the oil and sauce.
  2. put the chicken in the bag and mix it very well so all of the pieces get marinated
  3. leave in the fridge overnight
