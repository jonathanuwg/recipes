Karen's 'Flying D' Chili

by Jim Herbert, courtesy of Ted's Montana Grill

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450 g) ground bison
  • 1/2 cup (125 mL) diced yellow onions
  • 2 tsp (10 mL) minced garlic
  • 1-1/2 cups (375 mL) beef stock
  • 1/4 cup (60 mL) + 2 tbsp (30 mL) tomato paste
  • 1/2 tsp (2 mL) dried oregano leaves
  • 1 tbsp (15 mL) + 1 tsp (5 mL) chili powder
  • 1-1/4 tsp (6 mL) ground cumin
  • 1 tsp (5 mL) granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp (5 mL) Kosher salt
  • Pinch cayenne pepper
  • Pinch black pepper
  • 1/2 cup (125 mL) canned ranch style pinto beans, rinsed, drained
  • 1/2 cup (125 mL) canned diced tomato
  • Optional toppings:
    • Shredded cheddar cheese
    • Finely diced red onions
    • Chopped jalapenos

Directions

  1. In a large pot over medium-high heat, cook bison, stirring with a wooden spoon to break up large pieces, 5 minutes.
  2. Add the onions and garlic.
  3. Cook, stirring, until soft, about 5 to 7 minutes.
  4. In a measuring cup, combine the tomato paste and beef stock. Whisk well. Add to the pot and bring to a boil.
  5. Add the chili powder, oregano, cumin, sugar, salt, cayenne and black pepper.
  6. Reduce heat to medium-low. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  7. Add the beans and tomatoes. Simmer for 1 minute.
  8. Serve chili topped, if desired, with cheese, onions and jalapenos.
  9. Makes 2 to 3 servings.
